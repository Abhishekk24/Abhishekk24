### About

I'm a software engineer based in Mumbai, India. Currently the **Lead Software Developer at [CyreneAI](https://cyreneai.com)**, where I build decentralized AI agent infrastructure on Solana — working across DePIN, tokenization, and autonomous agent systems. I also contributed to **[Stories.fun](https://stories.fun)**, a Web3 storytelling platform.

Previously, I worked as a Lead SDE Intern at Star Union Dai-Ichi Life Insurance, building AI-powered avatars and enterprise web solutions. I hold a **B.E. in Information Technology (CGPA: 9.23)** and enjoy working at the intersection of Web3, AI, and systems programming. Most of my recent work is in **TypeScript**, **Rust**, and **Solana**.
